Jo Tera Daaman Chhut Jayega
Jo tera daaman chhut jayega, Toh main bhi kahaan sambhal paunga,
Saanson mein teri khushboo hai, Kaise main khud ko samjhaunga?
Teri yaadon ka silsila jo toota, Toh har lamha adhoora lagega,
Mere khwabon ka aashiyana bhi, Bina tere adhura sa lagega.
- Mrityunjay Bhardwaj

Dil Ki Baat
Dil ki baat lab tak aati hai,
Phir bhi khamoshi rehti hai,
Tum samajh kar bhi na samjho toh,
Mohabbat aur bhi gehri hai.
- Mrityunjay Bhardwaj
